About Me

Hi! I'm Pooya Alehossein, a third-year Computer Science student with a minor in Mathematics at George Mason University.

Beyond code, I have a deep interest in esports, music production, and photography. I enjoy building custom PCs and exploring how technology and creativity intersect in everyday life.

I currently work as a Learning Assistant for MATH 213 (Calculus III), where I help students strengthen their understanding of advanced mathematical concepts.

One of my greatest strengths is my ability to collaborate across disciplines—whether it's teaming up with engineers, artists, or designers, I thrive in environments where diverse perspectives come together. Creativity and teamwork are the heart of my approach to game development.
