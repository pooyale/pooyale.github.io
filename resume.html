We're Ducked

Two-player cooperative survival game. Stranded on a sinking island known for its unpredictable weather, two ducks must scavenge for resources, build shelter, and survive escalating storms until rescue arrives. Random events—including rescuing ducklings, avoiding wildlife, and solving environmental puzzles—keep players engaged as they work together under pressure to make it out alive.
